Our Mission & History

Mission

Our mission is to provide the best Fastpitch experience in Canada. Competitive and fun games that are well organized and professional. We aim to encourage participation by all in a sport that rewards athleticism in all areas of the game.

History

CISA has been in Calgary since 1968. In that time the league has been a strong home to Fastpitch from the recreational to the international level. We have had and continue to have numerous players who compete at the highest levels.

If you were a part of the league in its past and have a story to tell, we would love to share it here. Please email webmaster@calgaryfastpitch.com with any stories/pictures you have.
