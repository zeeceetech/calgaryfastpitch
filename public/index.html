Welcome to CISA

CISA hosts many youth and adult events through Softball Alberta and Minor Softball. Join us for exciting tournaments, community events, and competitive fastpitch action throughout the season!

    CISA also hosts the Mens and Womens Calgary Fastpitch League We have 12 mens and 4 ladies teams competing with a full season from May to October including playoffs.

What We Offer

Experience Premier Fastpitch Softball in Calgary

Quality facilities and competitive play for all skill levels

Multiple Leagues

Men's and women's leagues with teams across various skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, we have a place for you.

Tournaments

Annual tournaments including Spring Fling, Stampede Stomp, Williams Cup featuring teams from across the region.

Quality Facilities

Three well-maintained diamonds at Crawford Fields with fencing, covered spectator areas and a great concession.

Full Season

Enjoy competitive fastpitch from spring through summer with regular season games, playoffs, and championship tournaments.

Community Focus

Building lasting friendships and community connections through our love of fastpitch softball. All ages and skill levels welcome!

Competitive Play

Multiple divisions ensure balanced competition. Play against teams at your skill level while having the chance to advance.
