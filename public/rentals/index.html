Our Facility

Calgary Industrial Diamond Rentals

At CISA we take great pride in our facility and when it's not being used for league activities, it's available for rentals. We have 3 top notch fields that are fully fenced, restrooms, change rooms, kitchen, spectator areas with overhead safety netting, and a small play area for kids. All dugouts are enclosed and we also have a scoring area with viewing of all diamonds.

For more information about renting our park, contact rentals@calgaryfastpitch.com
